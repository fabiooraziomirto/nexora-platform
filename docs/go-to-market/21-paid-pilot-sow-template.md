# Paid Pilot SOW Template

## Pilot Summary
- Duration: 30 days.
- Scope: one audited remote execution or rollout use case.
- Fleet size: up to 25 devices.
- Environment: customer staging or controlled production subset.
- Support: weekly review plus async support channel.

## Success Criteria
- Devices onboarded in under 15 minutes median.
- Execution success rate >= 95% for agreed safe commands.
- Dispatch p95 <= 2 seconds in pilot environment or documented mitigation.
- Audit bundle produced for every weekly review.
- Customer identifies expansion use case or blocker by day 30.

## Nexora Responsibilities
- Provide setup guidance and pilot runbook.
- Configure identity, roles, and initial operators.
- Run demo reliability gate before kickoff.
- Provide weekly KPI report and audit evidence export.
- Document blockers and mitigations.

## Customer Responsibilities
- Provide pilot operator and technical champion.
- Provide test devices or emulator acceptance.
- Approve safe command/runbook scope.
- Attend weekly review.
- Provide go/no-go decision at pilot close.

## Commercial Terms Placeholder
- Pilot fee: TBD, paid.
- Discount policy: pilot fee may be credited to annual subscription if converted within 60 days.
- Expansion options: Starter, Pro, Enterprise packages.

## Out of Scope
- Unbounded custom integrations.
- Unsafe/destructive remote commands.
- Production SLA beyond the agreed pilot support model.
- Enterprise compliance commitments not listed in the pilot scope.
