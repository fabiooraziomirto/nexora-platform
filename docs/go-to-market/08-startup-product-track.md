# Startup Product Track (vs Publication Track)

## Decision
Nexora is managed as a startup product initiative, not as an academic publication outcome.

## 90-Day Product-Market Focus
- Convert technical validation into paid pilot evidence.
- Narrow ICP and use cases to reduce sales cycle friction.
- Build repeatable onboarding and demo-to-pilot conversion.
- Produce investor-grade operating metrics.

## ICP and Beachhead
- Primary ICP: industrial operations teams with distributed edge devices.
- Buyer: Head of Operations / CTO in SMB-midmarket industrial tech.
- Champion: platform engineer / IoT lead.
- Beachhead use case: remote execution and audited rollout on small fleets (10-100 devices).

## Startup KPIs (Weekly)
- Funnel:
  - 10 qualified outbound touches per week
  - 3 discovery calls booked per week
  - 1 technical demo completed per week
- Revenue:
  - 2 paid pilots in 60 days
  - target pilot ACV range documented per segment
- Product:
  - median onboarding <= 15 minutes
  - dispatch p95 <= 2s
  - end-to-end success rate >= 95%
- Delivery:
  - P0 open count = 0
  - P1 open count <= 1 with mitigation

## Commercial Packaging (Initial)
- Starter: single-site pilot, up to small fleet, limited SLA.
- Pro: multi-site operations, audit/export, higher limits.
- Enterprise: SSO/RBAC hardening, private networking, support plan.

## 30-Day Execution Add-ons
- Week 1: reliability + baseline (already running).
- Week 2: security profile for paid pilots and procurement checklist.
- Week 3: packaging + pricing experiments on 3 prospects.
- Week 4: close first pilot contract and launch onboarding.

## Evidence Pack for Customers and Investors
- Baseline KPI report and benchmark artifacts.
- 10-minute demo runbook + fallback API checks.
- Security one-pager and tenant-isolation evidence.
- Pilot SOW template and onboarding plan.

## Decision Gates
- Gate A (Day 30): at least 2 active pilot negotiations.
- Gate B (Day 60): first paid pilot signed and running.
- Gate C (Day 90): repeatable pipeline with defined CAC assumptions.
